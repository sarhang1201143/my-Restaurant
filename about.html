Our Story

1990

The Beginning

Sarhang Restaurant started as a small family kitchen in the heart of Erbil, serving traditional Kurdish recipes passed down through generations.

2005

New Location

We moved to our iconic location on 100 Meter Street, expanding our capacity to welcome more guests and families.

2015

Award Winning

Recognized as the "Best Traditional Cuisine" in sulaimany, solidifying our place in the culinary map of Kurdistan.

2024

Modern Era

A complete renovation to blend modern dining comfort with our authentic traditional atmosphere.

Meet Our Chef

Chef Azad

With over 25 years of experience in Kurdish and Middle Eastern cuisine, Chef Azad brings passion and perfection to every plate. His philosophy is simple: use fresh, local ingredients and never compromise on tradition. From the perfectly spiced kofta to the delicate layers of baklava, Chef Azad ensures every bite tells a story of Erbil's rich culinary heritage.
